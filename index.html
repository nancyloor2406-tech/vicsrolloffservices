Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much does a dumpster rental cost near me?

Dumpster rental prices vary based on your location, the size of the container, the type of debris, and the rental duration. Generally, a residential dumpster rental can range from $300 to $800. For the most accurate quote, including dumpster rental prices near me, please contact us directly with your zip code and project details.

2. What dumpster sizes do you have available?

We offer a wide range of sizes to suit any project, including 10 yard dumpsters, 15 yard dumpsters, 20 yard dumpsters, 30 yard dumpsters, and 40 yard dumpsters. Whether you need a mini dumpster rental for a small cleanup or a large construction dumpster rental, we have the inventory to meet your needs.

3. What items are prohibited in a roll off dumpster?

While you can dispose of most household and construction debris in our waste container rental, certain hazardous materials are prohibited. This typically includes tires, batteries, paint, asbestos, chemicals, and flammable liquids. Please consult with us regarding waste management bin rental policies to ensure compliance with local regulations.

4. How long can I keep the rental dumpster?

Our standard rental period is typically 7 to 14 days, but we offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your project timeline. If you need the trash container rental for a longer period, simply let us know, and we can adjust the rental terms. We strive to be the most flexible dumpster company near me.

5. How high can I load the dumpster?

For safety and transport reasons, you must not fill the waste bin rental above the top rim ("water level"). Overloading the container can create road hazards and may result in additional fees or a refusal to pick up the roll off dumpster.
